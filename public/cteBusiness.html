Introduction to Marketing

Through project-based learning, students learn the functions of marketing and the infrastructure of the business world. Students receive an overview of core business concepts, including business environment, ethical practices, business ownership, management, communication and principles of economics. This practical course also teaches students to be able to sell the most important product: themselves.

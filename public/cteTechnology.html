Applied Photography

This is an advanced level photography class for students who wish to review and refine the skills introduced in Photography 1. Manual shooting mode, traditional and creative studio lighting techniques, and other new photographic techniques are explored through projects to build an exemplary portfolio. Students study and apply the influence of professional photographers to their own work.

Architectural Drawing

Students learn drawing skills, room design, rendering, model building, building codes and structural design, spending substantial time learning to use computer animated design software (CAD). Advanced level students, planning on a career in a related field, learn more about energy-efficient construction, cost estimation, and codes. Students work to build an exemplary portfolio.

Biotech & Global Health

Students explore headlining global health topics, including cloning, stem cell research, ethics, and personalized medicine, with an emphasis on hands-on laboratory training in the techniques used in global health, biotechnology and genetic engineering today. Students are also given the opportunity to explore career options in the biotechnology field.

Intro to Computer Science

Students delve into the leading field of computer science by creating their own games and programming a robot. Students study the History and Applications of CS, Computational Thinking, Boolean Logic, Recursion, Input/Output (I/O), Algorithms and Data Structures, Human-Computer Interaction, Computer Engineering, Programming Languages, and Problem abstraction.

AP Computer Science A

In this course, students design and implement solutions to computer science problems by writing, running, and debugging computer programs using the programming language Java. The course emphasizes object-oriented programming methodology with a concentration on problem solving and algorithm development. Dedicated Microsoft TEALS volunteers tutor students daily.

Materials Science

Over the course of this class, students learn about metals, ceramics, polymers (plastics), and composites through careful observations, creating, questioning, and building. This engineering course involves welding, casting metals into molds, working glass, cutting polymers, and much more. This class is great for students who love hands on classes and lab science.

Mechanical Engineering

Approximately 50% of students' in-class time is spent developing skills using CAD systems such as AutoCAD, Rhinoceros, sketchup, and other software that introduces students to engineering graphics. CAD/CAM software, laser cutters, 3D printers are used to develop and transfer ideas into 3D parts, models, and projects. Students create a portfolio presented according to professional standards.

Yearbook

This class is a production based class involving photography, graphic design, writing, marketing and research. Its highly motivated and creative students create exemplary yearbooks to be loved by students and alumni for years and enjoy the satisfaction of seeing their work in print.

Theatre Lighting & Sound

Students learn the skills and techniques of theatrical lighting and sound design through hands-on coursework. The curriculum addresses all aspects of the design and functional use of stage lights and sound in the Theatre space, including the newest technology: intelligent lighting and moving lights. Students work on lighting and sound in connection with Juanita drama productions.

Theatre Costume & Scenic Design

Theatre Costume/Scenic Design is a hands-on theatre course that teaches the skills and techniques of costume and set design. Students of all experience levels learn how to budget, design, plan and build costume and set pieces, and have the opportunity to work on costumes and sets in connection with Juanita drama productions.

Production Workshop

Students in Production Workshop support the drama productions, club meetings, festivals, and other theatre activities. Students also audition for and participate in the year's productions, which involves some extended after-school time for rehearsals.

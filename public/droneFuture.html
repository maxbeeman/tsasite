Drones: th Future

Read the text below for more information!

Drone technology will continue to improve in the coming years. We will be seeing advances in every facet of their existence - lengthened flight periods, better stability, increased maneuverability, improved sensors, and declining costs. It is also expected that drones will begin to be combined with other technology, including 3D printers and solar panels. Slowly but surely, or maybe quickly, drones will become more and more abundant in our everyday lives. Drones could soon be delivering groceries, carrying the mail, monitoring the atmosphere, exploring the solar system to a greater extent, and who knows what else! The one thing I am really excited for is the day a drone carries my backpack.

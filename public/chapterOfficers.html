President
Olivia Williams

Olivia has been president of Juanita TSA for three of her four high school years, and was vice president the fourth. She's been an avid member of TSA since 7th grade. A senior at JHS, Olivia plans to study scientific journalism in college.

Vice President
Maxine Beeman

Maxine has served in a myriad of leadership roles since joining TSA in 9th grade. A senior at JHS, Maxine hopes to study electrical engineering at MIT.

Secretary
David Brostek

David, a member of TSA since junior high, is excited to be a member of this year's leadership. A junior at JHS, he plans to study computer science.

Diego has previously served as JHS's Sergeant at Arms. A TSA member since 10th grade and a Juanita senior, he plans to study mechanical engineering and music composition.

A member of TSA since his sophomore year and a senior at Juanita, Fedor loves working toward each year's state competition. He's excited to be a member of this year's leadership and plans to study chemistry in college.

Juanita High School Chapter Activities

Club Activity: Paper Rockets

One of this year's favorite club activities was the paper rocket competition, inspired by an exhibit at the 2015 Seattle Maker Fair! New and returning TSA members joined club advisor Mr. Shelton in September to design, construct, and launch paper rockets in the Juanita parking lot.

Club Activity: Problem Solving

TSA members explored the applications of geometry this year by folding large index cards into beams capable of supporting up to 15 lbs! Students competed to find the strongest and simplest beam design and practice their problem solving skills.

Regional Conference

Juanita students practice their problem solving and structural design skills in Finn Hill Middle School's annual regional conference, as well as encourage middle school students to continue as members of TSA through high school.

Club Activity: Yearlong Projects

TSA students spend most of the school year until April working on projects for the annual state conference. Students design and create items for a myriad of different competitions, including video game design, desktop publishing, biotechnology design, and fashion design.

State Conference

Juanita TSA students spend a busy and fun-filled March weekend every year at Washington's annual TSA state conference. Each year, students compete in conference competitions including prepared and extemporaneous speech and photographic technology.

Movie Night Fundraiser

Juanita TSA hosts movie night fundraisers featuring popular movies such as Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope and Guardians of the Galaxy. Movie nights are held in the Juanita commons on a Friday evening once a semester. Snacks and beverages are offered free to everyone who attends, though donations are suggested! These donations to go towards student scholarships to help members to attend the annual state conference and towards covering the cost of meeting activity and competition supplies.

FTC Robotics Demonstration

TSA invited a local FIRST Technology Challenge team to demonstrate their robot and give advice to Juanita's budding VEX robotics team. Both the FTC team and a former LWHS VEX robotics coach offered handy tips to Juanita's VEX team.

Technology Tutoring

TSA students offer to tutor Juanita's AP Computer Science students after school during our Thursday club meetings.

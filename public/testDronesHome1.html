Definitions

UAS

Unmanned Aerial System; term that describes the unmanned aircraft paired with its ground-based controller, and the communication system connecting the two. Commands can come from either a human or computer.

UAV

Unmanned Aerial Vehicle; specifically the unmanned aircraft.

Drone

Used as synonym for both UAV and UAS.

(Note: In the media, these terms are used interchangeably.)
